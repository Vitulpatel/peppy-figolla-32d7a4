About Us

A Family Legacy
in Hospitality

Founded by Hersil and Vitul Patel, Yogi Management Inc. is a privately held hospitality corporation headquartered in Sioux Falls, South Dakota.

What began as a single vision has grown into a multi-state portfolio of premier limited-service hotels — built on the belief that every guest deserves a home away from home.
